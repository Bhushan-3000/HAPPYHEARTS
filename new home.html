    At HappyHearts, We take care of...


    Anxiety & Worries

    Anxiety is a persistent state of fear or apprehension, often without a clear cause, and can include physical symptoms like increased heart rate and sweating, while **worry** is a temporary, specific response to uncertain situations.

    Depression & Mood

    Depression is a mood disorder characterized by persistent sadness and loss of interest in activities, affecting how you feel, think, and behave, while mood refers to a temporary emotional state that can vary from happiness to sadness

    Behavioral Difficulties

    Behavioral difficulties refer to patterns of disruptive behaviors that interfere with a personâ€™s ability to function in daily life, affecting their interactions at home, school, or work. These difficulties can include issues like impulsivity, aggression, defiance, and problems with attention

    Adjustments & Transitions

    Adjustments and transitions in children refer to the emotional and behavioral responses children have when facing changes, such as moving to a new home, starting a new school, or changes in family dynamics. These transitions can lead to stress and require time and support for children to adapt
